Our Mission

Cutting-edge technologies that involve artificial intelligence and robotics are emerging these years. Companies such as Google, IBM, Microsoft, Boston Dynamics, Uber, and Autodesk are all contributing to the frontier of science and technology. However, this branch of science should not be limited to the research industry. The mission of our club is to educate high school students about artificial intelligence and robotics and help students develop innovative engineering skills.

What do you want to achieve in this club?

  • Learn to code
  • Build a drone
  • Using a 3D printer
  • Build a website
  • Create a phone app
